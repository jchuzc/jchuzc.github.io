USS Hornet Aircraft Carrier

Served during World War 2.

The USS Hornet (CV‑12) is now permanently berthed as a museum ship in Alameda, California, and is open to the public as the USS Hornet Sea, Air & Space Museum. It is widely known for its historic role in recovering the Apollo 11 and 12 Moon missions.
